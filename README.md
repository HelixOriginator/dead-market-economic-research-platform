The Dead Market – Economic Research Platform

The Dead Market is an independent economic research platform exploring global market dynamics, systemic risk, behavioral finance, crypto assets, and policy impact.

Created by Kallol Chakrabarti, Global Independent Researcher.

Overview

The Dead Market examines overlooked sectors, declining markets, and structural inefficiencies across global financial systems. The platform combines economic theory, quantitative analysis, and behavioral research to uncover hidden patterns in capital flows and market cycles.

This project serves as the official website interface for publishing research insights, visual analysis, and data-driven commentary.
