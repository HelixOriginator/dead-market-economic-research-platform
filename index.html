About the Market

Understanding the Dead Market Phenomenon

The Dead Market is an independent economic research platform dedicated to exploring the overlooked corners of global financial markets. The platform uncovers hidden patterns, systemic inefficiencies, and economic phenomena that mainstream financial analysis frequently misses.

Founded by Kallol Chakrabarti — a global independent researcher working across financial market analysis, quantitative data science, and economic theory — The Dead Market operates fully independently, unaffiliated with any financial institution, brokerage, or government body. Its commitment to open, transparent, and academically rigorous research dissemination underpins every aspect of the platform.
